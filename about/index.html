关于我 / About Me

My name is Lee Jing(李靖). You can call me Barret. I was born in 1992.

I am a Bachelor of Engineering, graduated from HuaZhong University Of Science and Technology, and now am employed by Alibaba in HangZhou.

Life is colorful, this website was, is and will be the window which used to present the color of my life.
